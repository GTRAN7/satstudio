About the Cause

Proceeds support educational equity initiatives in Vietnam

More Than Tutoring: A Mission

My journey with House of Wisdom began when I witnessed firsthand how many children in Vietnam didn't even have the chance to go to school—an opportunity that many of us take for granted. Since then, I've been actively working with this remarkable organization to help expand access to education across rural and underprivileged areas in Vietnam.

Last summer, I taught several online classes to students through House of Wisdom, which deepened my commitment when I saw the challenges these eager learners face daily. Despite their enthusiasm, many centers had severely limited resources, with students crowded around a single small screen just to participate in lessons.

House of Wisdom has already established over 350 active centers and built more than 20,000 bookshelves and libraries throughout Vietnam, but there's still so much more to do. I'm dedicating 50% of all proceeds from this SAT tutoring program to continue advancing this cause.

By choosing this tutoring service, you're not just investing in better SAT scores—you're joining me on a mission to bridge the educational divide.
